## Active Channels

### 1. Facebook Multi-Group Posting (Organic)

**What**: Post professional recruitment and task-related content to multiple Bulgarian Facebook groups simultaneously using Meta Business Suite Composer.

**Composer URL**:
```
https://business.facebook.com/latest/composer?asset_id=913766661815277&business_id=868541485924722&nav_ref=fb_composer_share_to_groups_redirect_composer&ref=fb_composer_share_to_groups
```

**How**:
- Write a post (RU/BG depending on audience)
- Select multiple groups to post to at once
- Include a deep registration link (see CLAUDE.md for link format)

**Tips**:
- Vary post text between groups to avoid spam detection
- Post at peak hours (9-11am, 7-9pm Bulgarian time)
- Mix professional recruitment posts with customer-facing posts

---

### 2. Facebook Comments on Relevant Posts (Organic)

**What**: Monitor Bulgarian Facebook groups where people ask for professionals (e.g., "Търся майстор", "Нужен ми е електрик") and reply with a Trudify deep link.

**How**:
- Join relevant groups (home repair, city groups, expat groups)
- Search for keywords: "търся", "нужен", "препоръчайте", "ищу", "нужен мастер"
- Reply helpfully with a link to Trudify (either to browse professionals or to post a task)

**Tips**:
- Be helpful, not spammy — answer the question first, then mention Trudify
- Generate deep registration links matching the post language and profession (see CLAUDE.md)
- For Russian-speaking groups, use `/ru/` links; for Bulgarian, use `/bg/`

---

### 3. Telegram Leads Search (Organic)

**What**: Find professionals in Bulgarian Telegram groups/channels and invite them to register.

**How**:
- Search Telegram for Bulgarian professional groups (ремонт, майстори, работа)
- Direct message professionals with a personalized invite + registration link
- Share in relevant Telegram channels

**Tips**:
- Personalize the message — mention their specific skill
- Keep it short and value-focused (free, no commission, get clients)

---

### 4. Meta Video Ads (Paid)
